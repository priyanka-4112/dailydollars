What is The Click Engine, Exactly?

The Click Engine is a "traffic-on-tap" service created by Jeff Aman. Its purpose is to provide affiliate marketers and business owners a hands-free way to get real buyer traffic without the high cost and complexity of paid advertising or solo ad vendors.

It is not a course, but a done-for-you service. Users submit a link, which is then added to a traffic rotator, sending a stream of real people to their offer. The concept is especially appealing for those new to digital marketing or struggling to generate consistent traffic.
